The Solstice Way

Beyond the Object

Founded in the heart of South Africa, Solstice began with a desire to return to the essentials. We believe that the objects we invite into our homes should be chosen with intention and crafted to last generations.

Our collection is a dialogue between modern utility and the natural world. Every piece is sourced ethically, ensuring that our footprint is as light as the aesthetic we champion.
